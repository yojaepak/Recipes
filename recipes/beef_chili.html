Beef Chili

Description

A slow cooked delicious beef chili for the cold winter days. Perfect for one or more!

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14.5 oz) Wegmans Petite Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 can (28 oz) Wegmans Crushed Tomatoes
  • 4 cloves Wegmans Organic Peeled Garlic, minced
  • 1 white onion, peeled, 1/4-inch dice (about 2 cups)
  • 1 organic green bell pepper, cored, seeded, 1/4-inch dice (about 1 cup)
  • 1 jalapeno, cored, seeded, 1/4-inch dice (wear gloves when handling)
  • 1 lb Wegmans Organic 80/20 Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 can (15 oz) Wegmans Organic Cannellini Beans, rinsed, drained
  • 2 cups Wegmans Organic Beef Bone Broth
  • 1 container (2.08 oz) Wegmans Organic Chili Seasoning
  • 1 Tbsp hot chili powder
  • 1 Tbsp Wegmans Organic Ground Cumin
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Add tomatoes, garlic, onion, peppers, jalapenos, ground beef, beans, broth, chili powders, cumin, and salt to slow-cooker. Stir to combine; cover.
  2. Cook on HIGH 4 hours or LOW 8 hours.
