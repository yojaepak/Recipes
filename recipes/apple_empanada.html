Apple Sausage and Cheese Empanada

Description

A savory and sweet treat that combines apples, sausage, and cheese. All in a single empanada!

Ingreditents

  • 1 tsp + 1 Tbsp Wegmans Organic Basting Oil
  • 1/2 lb (2 links) Italian Classics Mild Italian Sausage, casings removed
  • 1/2 Wegmans Organic Yellow Onions, peeled, chopped (about 1 cup)
  • 2 (1/2 lb each) firm apples, peeled, cored, chopped (about 4 cups)
  • 1 Tbsp chopped Wegmans Organic Italian Parsley
  • 1 pinch Wegmans Organic Ground Cumin
  • 6 oz Wegmans 12 Month Aged Mild Cheddar Cheese (Cheese Shop), shredded (about 2 cups)
  • 1 Wegmans Organic Large Brown Egg
  • 1 can (10 biscuits) Pillsbury Buttermilk Flaky Layers Biscuits (Dairy Dept)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Heat 1 tsp basting oil in large nonstick pan on MED-HIGH until oil faintly smokes. Add sausage; cook, stirring to break up pieces, 3-4 min until browned. Remove from pan; set aside.
  2. Reduce heat to MED. Add 1 Tbsp basting oil and onion to pan; cook, stirring, 2-3 min. Add apples, cumin, and browned sausage. Cook, stirring occasionally, 12-14 min until apples are softened.
  3. Remove from heat; stir in cheese and parsley.
  4. Lightly beat egg with few drops water in small bowl.
  5. Roll out each biscuit on lightly floured surface to 4-inch round; brush edges with egg. Top with 1/4 cup filling; fold over biscuit, enclosing filling. Seal edges by pressing down firmly with tines of fork; brush with egg. Repeat with remaining biscuits and filling. Arrange on greased baking sheet.
  6. Bake 15 min until golden brown.
